About

This tool is designed to estimate your probability of being infected with COVID-19 by attending class in person at the top 18 universities in the U.S. (according to U.S. News & World Report). You can also find plenty of general information about the COVID-19 pandemic and how to stay safe as well! On this site you can find:

  • Risk Calculator will help you find your infection probability and explain the underlying assumptions of the algorithm. All you need to do is be able to estimate the dimensions of your classroom, the amount of time you will be in class, and the number of students who will likely attend class
  • University Policies where you can discover what the top universities in the U.S. are doing to protect their students on and off campus
  • CDC Guidelines works to provide a short summary of the best practices for safety during these times directly from the Center for Disease Control or CDC
  • Infection Probability will display your probability of infection after you have utilized the Risk Calculator
